TOKYO QUICK CITY GUIDE — JULY 18–25, 2026

Shinjuku
- Explore the observation deck and evening food alleys.
- Allow half a day; easiest by metro.

Shibuya
- Visit Shibuya Crossing and nearby shops.
- Good evening stop after Harajuku.

Asakusa
- Visit Sensō-ji early in the morning to avoid crowds.
- Browse Nakamise-dori and walk toward the Sumida River.

Travel notes
- Pick up an IC transit card after arriving at HND.
- Consider a rail pass only if adding travel outside Tokyo.
- July is hot and humid; carry water and plan indoor breaks.
